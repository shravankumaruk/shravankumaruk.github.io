Welcome to Mangalore City Police

Welcome to the official website of Mangalore City Police , Government of Karnataka.

Mangalore City Police is the law enforcement agency responsible for maintaining law and order in the city of Mangalore, located in the southern state of Karnataka, India. With a rich history and a commitment to ensuring the safety and security of its residents, the Mangalore City Police play a vital role in maintaining a peaceful environment within the city. Mangalore City Police are known for their efforts in embracing technology and innovation to enhance their operations. This includes the implementation of modern surveillance systems, traffic management solutions, and online platforms for citizen engagement and reporting of incidents. By leveraging these technological advancements, they aim to provide more effective and efficient services to the public.
